Overcoming Challenges in India

Whenever we travel there are some challenges that come up. While travelling to India although I am fimilar with the plcae there still were many problems that came up. It takes you time to adapt to things happening around you.

Challenges About
The hot and humid weather

The heat was intense, and it took me a while to get used to it. Staying hydrated and wearing lightweight clothes helped me manage the weather.

Bustling cities

The bustling streets of India can sometimes be overwhelming with their chaos, noise, and crowds. The constant honking of car horns and the crowd of people can make it hard to focus. However, I soon learned to embrace the chaos as part of India's charm and a reflection of its vibrant energy.

Time zone changed

The time zones are different in Canada and India. It took me a few days to adapt to the new time zone, and the jet lag was tough at first.
