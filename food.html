Delicious Indian Dishes

India is famous for the flavourful and delicious food. I personally loved the street food cause it brings back memories from childhood. The spicy, sweet and sour dishes make your experiences extra beutiful. Plus sometimes where the foos stalls there would a buetiful view to enjoy. For example in Mumbai, you would be near the Marine Drive eating enjoying the sea and sunset.
Here is a list of the food I loved the most.

  1. Dal Batti
  2. Vada Pav
  3. Gol Gappe a.k.a Pani Puri
  4. Chole Bature
  5. Misal Pav
  6. Samosa
  7. Jalebi
  8. Kachori
  9. Dosa

This was all the street food I loved but how can we miss talking about the famous Taj Hotel in Mumbai. The hotel is famous for the elite experience and fine dining. It could be a once in a lifetime experince to eat there. You might even get the chance to meet some famous personalities and chefs.
